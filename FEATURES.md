# What You Can Do With HyperEdit

Everything you can do, explained simply.

---

## The Basics

### Upload a Video
Click the **+** button in the left panel or drag a video file into the app. Your video appears in the asset library and can be dragged onto the timeline.

### Edit on the Timeline
- **Drag** clips to move them
- **Drag the edges** of a clip to trim it
- **Right-click** a clip for options (split, delete, duplicate)
- The timeline has 6 tracks: captions on top, 3 video layers, 2 audio layers

### Preview Your Video
The big preview window in the centre shows your video. Click play or press Space to watch. The red line shows where you are in the timeline.

### Export Your Video
Click the **Export** button in the top right. Choose your platform (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter) and it renders the final video for you to download.

## AI Features (The Good Stuff)

### Talk to the AI Director
The chat panel on the right is your AI assistant. Just type what you want in plain English:

- "Remove all the silent parts"
- "Add captions to this video"
- "Speed this up to 1.5x"
- "Make it look cinematic"
- "Generate chapters for YouTube"
- "Add a call to action animation"

It understands what you mean and does it.

### One-Click Recipes
Instead of typing, click a recipe button:
- **YouTube-Ready** - does everything to make it YouTube-perfect
- **Podcast Polish** - cleans audio, adds chapters
- **Social Clip** - crops and optimises for social media
- **Tutorial** - adds zooms, captions, and chapters

### Auto-Captions
The AI listens to your video, creates word-by-word captions, and puts them on screen. Choose from 6 animation styles: karaoke, fade, pop, bounce, typewriter, or plain.

It also removes filler words (um, uh, like, you know) from the captions automatically.

### Dead Air Removal
One click removes all the awkward silences. The AI detects quiet moments and cuts them out, making your video tighter and more engaging.

### Chapter Generation
Analyses your video and creates YouTube chapters automatically. Copy and paste them into your YouTube description.

## Create Graphics and Animations

### Motion Graphics Templates
11 ready-made templates you can add to your video:
- **Animated Text** - fancy text that moves
- **Lower Third** - name/title bar at the bottom
- **Call to Action** - subscribe buttons, links
- **Counter** - animated number counting up
- **Logo Reveal** - your logo appears with effects
- **Progress Bar** - shows how far through the video
- **Social Proof** - follower counts, ratings
- And more...

### AI-Generated Animations
Describe any animation you want:
- "A spinning globe with particles"
- "Text that types itself out letter by letter"
- "A chart showing growth from 0 to 1000"

The AI writes the code and renders it for you.

### Image Generation
Type a description, get an AI-generated image:
- "A futuristic city at sunset"
- "A robot working at a computer"
- "Abstract blue and purple background"

The image appears in your asset library ready to drag onto the timeline.

## Video Effects

### Image to Video
Turn any still image into a short animated video clip. The AI makes it move naturally.

### Restyle Video
Change the visual style of your video:
- Make it look like anime
- Make it look like a painting
- Make it look like a video game

### Remove Background
Remove the background from any video clip. Useful for overlay effects and green-screen style editing.

### GIF Auto-Insert
The AI watches your video, figures out what you're talking about, searches for relevant GIFs, and places them at the right moments. Great for meme-style editing.

---

## Advanced Features

### Reaction Videos
Combine two videos into a reaction-style layout:
- **Picture-in-Picture** - small video in the corner
- **Side by Side** - two videos next to each other
- **Top and Bottom** - one above the other

### Stock Footage Search
Search millions of free stock images and videos from Pexels and Pixabay. They download directly into your asset library ready to use.

### Auto B-Roll
Give the AI your video's transcript and it automatically finds relevant stock images to use as b-roll. One click and you have 5 images ready to place.

### Webpage Screenshot
Paste any website URL and get a screenshot as an image asset. Great for showing websites, articles, or social media posts in your video.

### Remotion Templates
Import pre-made video templates:
- **NewsClip** - breaking news style with image backgrounds
- **SplitScreen** - side by side comparison
- **FaceOverlay** - presenter with face cam bubble
- **HookTextVideo** - attention-grabbing text videos
- **ProductDemo** - product showcase
- And more...

Type your text, it renders the video, and drops it into your timeline.

## Tips

- **Always export a preview first** before doing the full export - it's much faster and lets you check everything looks right
- **Save your project regularly** - it auto-saves but better safe than sorry
- **Start with the AI recipes** if you're not sure what to do - they handle the basics for you
- **Use vertical (9:16) for TikTok/Reels** and horizontal (16:9) for YouTube
- **Shorter is better for social media** - aim for under 60 seconds for Reels/Shorts
